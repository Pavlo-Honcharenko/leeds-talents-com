Welcome to LEED Talents –
Your Partner for Remote Talent

Female Empowerment.

LEED Talents pioneers in Leadership, Empowerment, Employability, and Diversity. We are dedicated to addressing youth unemployment, migration challenges, and gender imbalances in the workforce, particularly in technical and digital fields. Our commitment extends beyond connecting young talents with opportunities; it‘s about making a lasting difference.

Bridging the Gender Gap
At LEED Talents, we specialize in aligning companies with top-tier ICT specialists, prioritizing female empowerment. Our unique recruitment strategy fills the crucial skills gap with talented professionals from Africa and Eastern Europe, who work remotely from our offices, thereby contributing to your digital transformation journey.

Enhanced Support and Development Programs
LEED Talents goes beyond the traditional talent acquisition model by providing comprehensive support and development:
1
AI and/or E-learning Assisted Assessment
Utilizing advanced AI tools, we ensure that our talent pool is pre-qualified, matching companies with candidates who not only meet but exceed their requirements. With our own e-learning platform LEED Academy with automated assessment tools, you can also carry out your own assessment tests with us.
2
Mentoring Program
We offer a six-month mentoring program to accompany both the talent and the company, ensuring smooth integration and expectation management.
3
Leadership Training
We give every talent a comprehensive leadership training designed to develop leadership strengths before the onboarding in your company. Here we enhance communication, negotiation, and decision-making skills, bolster self-confidence in navigating organizational dynamics and prepare for various working cultures.
4
Onboarding Coaching for Companies
For organizations new to managing remote workforces, we provide onboarding coaching to streamline the process.
5
LEED Academy
Our commitment to continuous development is embodied in the LEED Academy, offering further education opportunities to enhance both soft and hard skills, including language proficiency and specialized training tailored to your company‘s needs.
