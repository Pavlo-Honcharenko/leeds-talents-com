Hire the Talent of the
Future – And the
Future is Female.

At LEED Talents, we offer a unique partnership opportunity that goes beyond traditional talent acquisition, designed to cater specifically to the needs and goals of forward-thinking companies. Our approach is rooted in a deep understanding of the challenges and opportunities within the ICT sector, especially when it comes to fostering diversity and filling critical skills gaps.

Exceptional Talent from Underrepresented Regions
LEED Talents stands out by sourcing exceptional talent from underrepresented regions, focusing on female empowerment and technological innovation. Our candidates are not just skilled; they bring unparalleled motivation to their roles for several reasons:
Overcoming Barriers
Women in patriarchal societies often have to navigate additional hurdles to prove their worth in the tech industry. This journey fosters a level of determination and resilience that translates into exceptional performance and innovation when given the opportunity.
Above-Market Salaries
By providing salaries well above the market average, LEED Talents not only ensures the well-being of our talents but also enhances their motivation and loyalty to your projects.
International Exposure
The prospect of working for European and international companies is a significant motivator, offering our talents a sense of prestige, professional growth, and the opportunity to be part of the global tech community.
Social Impact
Our talents are driven by the knowledge that their work contributes not only to their personal success but also to broader social impact initiatives, reinforcing their commitment and enthusiasm.
Advantages for Your Company
Partnering with LEED Talents offers your company a myriad of strategic advantages:
Access to Pre-Qualified Talent
Through AI-assisted assessment and comprehensive leadership training, we ensure that you are matched with candidates who exceed your expectations in skill, cultural fit, and motivation.
Enhanced Diversity and Innovation
By integrating talents from diverse backgrounds, you enrich your team’s creativity and perspective, driving innovation and problem-solving.
Cost-Effective Solutions
Our model provides a cost-efficient way to scale your team with highly skilled professionals, without compromising on quality or commitment.
Support and Development Programs
From mentoring and onboarding coaching to continuous learning through the LEED Academy, we offer a suite of support services to ensure seamless integration and ongoing development of our talents within your organization.
Our Process
Engaging with LEED Talents is a straightforward process designed to align with your company‘s needs:
1
Consultation
We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your specific requirements, goals, and the roles you need to fill.
2
Matching
Utilizing our AI-assisted platform, we match you with pre-qualified talents who not only meet your criteria but are also highly motivated to succeed in your organization.
3
Mentoring & Onboarding Training
Our comprehensive support services, including mentoring, leadership training, and onboarding coaching, ensure a smooth transition for both the talent and your team.
4
Workspace Integration
Our local offices are hubs of innovation and collaboration, where talents sharing insights, brainstorming ideas, and solving complex problems together.
5
IT Integration
To meet our highest standards in Data security, we provide each employee with their own high-performance laptop, which already contains pre-installed software, guidelines and templates for reporting. The computers belong to LEED Talents and must be returned if an employee leaves the company. In this case, you as a company receive a complete backup of all data on this laptop before it is permanently deleted from the device.
6
Continious Development
Through the LEED Academy and tailored development programs, we facilitate ongoing skill enhancement and professional growth for our talents, ensuring they continue to contribute meaningfully to your projects.
